Nonnie's Meal Prepping service offers affordable, healthy, clean and lean portion controlled meals for all lifestyles.


We desire to help people embody an exciting approach to eating healthy and living healthy; a fun approach that is fueled
by great energy and even better food, so you can continue to live life to the fullest and make the best choices for you!


Below are just a couple of the different meal types we accomodate:


Keto

What is keto? “Ketogenic” is a term for a low-carb diet. The idea is for you to get more calories from protein and fat
and less from carbohydrates. You cut back most on the carbs that are easy to digest, like sugar, soda, pastries, and white bread.


Calorie Deficit

To lose weight, you need to eat and drink fewer calories than you burn. That's called a calorie deficit. If you take in more than
calories than you burn, then you don't have a calorie deficit and you won't lose weight. There are two ways to raise your calorie deficit:
changing what and how much you eat, and exercise.
